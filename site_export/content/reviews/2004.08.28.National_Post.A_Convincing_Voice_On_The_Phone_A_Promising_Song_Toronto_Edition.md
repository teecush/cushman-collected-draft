Jean Cocteau's The Human Voice meets the challenge of portraying one-half of a phone conversation. Lally Cadeau gives a seamless solo performance as a woman talking to her ex-lover. It's paired with The Elephant Song, a promising playwrighting debut by Nicholas Billon. Studio Theatre, Stratford, until Sept. 26. 1-800-567-1600, stratfordfestival.ca.
