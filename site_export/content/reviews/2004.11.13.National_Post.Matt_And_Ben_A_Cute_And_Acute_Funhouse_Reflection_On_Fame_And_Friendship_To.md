Matt and Ben is a sweet surprise. A one-act fantasia on how Matt Damon and Ben Affleck might have written Good Will Hunting (it literally fell into their laps) that's a funhouse reflection of the story it tells, since two New York actresses conceived it as a vehicle for themselves. Hilary Doyle and Jane Spence know the characters very well. It's acute and entertaining about the jealousies and loyalties of two young men who bonded as kids. The production is of exemplary crispness. (Poor Alex Theatre, ends Nov. 28. 416-324-9863.)
