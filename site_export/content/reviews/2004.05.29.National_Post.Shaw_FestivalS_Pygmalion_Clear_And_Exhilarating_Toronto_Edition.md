A treat. Shaw's comedy, an effortlessly witty hard-edged romance, is given a panoramic production, splendiferously designed, with Jim Mezon and Tara Rosling charting a clear and exhilarating line through the relationship of the professor and the flower-girl, and Simon Bradbury delivering dustman Doolittle's prose arias with superb aplomb. My Fair Lady hasnt killed this play off; theres room for both, and one wouldn't want to be without either. (Festival Theatre, Niagara-on-the-Lake, 1-800-511-7429. Runs until November.)
