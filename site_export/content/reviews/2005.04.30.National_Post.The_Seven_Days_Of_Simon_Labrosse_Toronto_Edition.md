Artword Alternative Space - 75 Portland St. - 416-366-7723

Carol Frechettes newest effort is more eventful than her other plays and quirkier, though the whimsy is sometimes close to unbearable. But it does provide a window into desperation. Simon, a young man who needs to get a life and a job, offers his services as an "emotional stuntman" to hostile strangers and pines for the girl of his dreams. In Michael Shamata's adroit production, Paul Fauteux is a stand-out as a perpetually angry friend. Ends tomorrow.
