This production has most of the musical and atmospheric trappings Tennessee Williams specified for his memory-play, but they come off more doomy than delicate. Rosemary Dunsmore's exaggerated Southern belle loses all sympathy, robbing the play of its emotional impact. With Michelle Monteith, Damien Atkins and Seann Gallagher. (Berkeley Street Downstairs. Until Feb. 16.)
