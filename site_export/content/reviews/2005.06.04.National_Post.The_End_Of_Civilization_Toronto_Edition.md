Factory Theatre - 125 Bathurst St. - 416-504-9971

As usual, George F. Walker's characters are at the end of their tethers, though here we can imagine them having been at the other end. Dennis O'Connor, a cross between Falstaff and Lear, plays a man, desperate through unemployment, whose railings have a farcical grandeur; Brenda Bazinet is his wife who discovers the easiest way to make money. Ron White and David Ferry are cops. The best play in Toronto, as bleak, funny and pertinent as ever. Through June 12.
