Factory Studio. - 125 Bathurst St.- 416-504-9971

Chris Earle's monologue is a vision of a Canada flooded by disillusioned soldiers and dissident celebrities ("every city got at least one Baldwin") from the United States. If the premise seems glib, Earle's writing can be both amusing and heartening, and his performing is spot-on. Through Feb. 5.
