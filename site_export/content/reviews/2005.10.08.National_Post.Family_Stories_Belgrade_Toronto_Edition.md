Harbourfront Studio - 231 Queens Quay W. - 416-973-4000

A Serbian play (by Biljana Srbljanovic) reflecting the country's '90s passion in what used to be called Absurdist terms: adult actors playing kids playing at being adults -- which can be confusing, and gets more so when the author starts playing her own games. Acting and direction (by the brilliant Aleksandar Sasha Lukac) are first- rate. Closes tonight.
