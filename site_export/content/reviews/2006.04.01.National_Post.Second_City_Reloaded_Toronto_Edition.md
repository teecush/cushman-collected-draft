The Second City - 51 Mercer St. - 416-343-0011

One of the troupe's best shows, with a manic quality we seldom see from them. Especially good are Anand Rajaram, as a Sikh taxi driver who discomfits his passenger by revealing a Scots accent, and Matt Baram, a walking nervous breakdown. Improv, previously confined to the end of the evening, now crops up within the show. Indefinite run.
