Noises Off, Michael Frayn's farce about the production of another farce (one that begins in chaos and goes downhill from there), is already a classic. The pace and timing of Brian Bedford's direction are impeccable. Some actors have trouble with the distinctively British idiom, but Barry MacGregor (who is British), Chick Reid and Sara Topham are dead-on. And Seana McKenna has a moment of worth-the-price-of-admission comic poetry. (Avon Theatre, Stratford. 1- 800-567-1600. stratfordfestival.ca. Runs until Oct. 30).
