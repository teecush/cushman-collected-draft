Factory Theatre - 125 Bathurst St. - 416-504-9971

Peter and Mary have been married for seven years, say theyre happy (so we know they're not), have a baby whose name they unbelievably never mention, and are rotten. In its own hermetic way Francois Archambault's play works, except for the notion that his characters' shenanigans constitute a critique of our acquisitive civilization. The play, with its implication that sexual experimentation is the privilege of the privileged, seems as snobbish as they are. Ends tomorrow.
