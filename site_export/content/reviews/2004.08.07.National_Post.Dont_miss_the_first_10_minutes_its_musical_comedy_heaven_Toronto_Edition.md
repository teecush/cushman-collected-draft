All toliet facilities have been handed over to a private corporation that runs them on a strict fee-for-use basis. A musical satire on matters political and environmental that's also a satire on the idea of doing a musical satire in the first place: not always as funny as it thinks it is but certainly as clever. The score is a super-pastiche that retains its own delighted voice. The performance standard is very high, and the first 10 minutes are musical-comedy heaven. (Bluma Appel Theatre, 25 Front St. E., 416-368-3110. Runs until Labour Day).
