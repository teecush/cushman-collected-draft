This lunchtime production of Terence Rattigan's very light satire on the theatre is great fun; though you may laugh as knowingly at the favourite actors playing it as at the actors they're playing. Peter Hutt is delicious as an ageing Romeo (literally) who looks like a superannuated buccaneer but is dedicated to his craft even in the grimmest farcical circumstances. There's a delightful performance from Jennifer Phipps as a tippling theatrical Dame. (Royal George Theatre, Niagara-on-the-Lake. Ends tomorrow. 800-511- 7429).
