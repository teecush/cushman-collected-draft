The Shaw Festival is currently rich in excellent young actors named Jeff: Jeff Lillico, Jeff Madden, Jeff Meadows, all of whom have given outstanding performances in the last week. (The company also has a couple of admirable Kevins.) This can occasionally cause confusion. In my review of Floyd Collins yesterday, I inadvertently attributed to Jeff Meadows a fine performance that is actually given by Jeff Madden. My apologies.
