Tarragon Extra Space - 30 Bridgman Ave. - 416-531-1827

The return of Morris Panych's play, possibly his best, about a paranoid recluse cursed by over-acute hearing. He can hear what everyone in his building is saying: A curse with benefits. On Ken MacDonald's vertiginously perspectived set, Randy Hughson gives one of the great Canadian performances. The talent is deafening. Through April 9.
