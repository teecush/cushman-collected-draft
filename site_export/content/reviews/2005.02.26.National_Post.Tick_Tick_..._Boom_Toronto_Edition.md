Poor Alex Theatre - 296 Brunswick Ave. * 905-881-6546

A fascinating fragment, written (and originally performed) by the late Jonathan Larson, six years before his breakthrough with Rent. The hero, Jon, is trying to blend Broadway and rock; meanwhile he waits tables and interacts with his girlfriend and roommate. The Toronto production isn't great, but what makes the evening is the songs, as driving, infectious and melodic as those in Rent, and much funnier. Ends tonight.
