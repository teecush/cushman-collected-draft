Peter Hinton's The Swanne is an allegory of the state of England, with special emphasis on its underside, purportedly written by the 18-year-old Queen Victoria, in muscular, often witty verse. As an old, bald, blind actress, Diane D'Aquila reaches greatness. Studio Theatre, Stratford, until Sept. 26. 800-567-1600, stratfordfestival.ca.
