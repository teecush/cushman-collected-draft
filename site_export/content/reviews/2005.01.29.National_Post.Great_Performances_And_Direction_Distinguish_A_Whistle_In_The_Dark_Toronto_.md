A flawless ensemble production of Tom Murphy's compelling play. Joseph Ziegler plays an Irish patriarch who has raised his sons, now living in England, on a code of violence; he is great. Scarcely less so are Jonathan Goad, Allan Hawko and Philip Riccio. Director Jason Byrne's work is the richest imaginable. (Berkeley Street Upstairs, 26 Berkeley St., 416-368-3110. Until Feb. 5.)
