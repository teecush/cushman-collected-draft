Kevin Bundy gives a captivating performance as a mild greeting- card poet with a gift for picking winners at the track. Unfortunately, much else in this "classic American comedy" suggests that those concerned don't trust the play. There are running gags at the expense of the material, and some performances are embarrassingly exaggerated, though the three gamblers who give the play its title make an excellently contrasted trio. (Festival Theatre, Niagara-on-the-Lake, 1-800-511-7429. Runs in repertory until October)
