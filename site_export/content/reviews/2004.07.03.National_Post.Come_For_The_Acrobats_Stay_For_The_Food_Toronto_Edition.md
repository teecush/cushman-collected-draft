Dancers from Cirque du Soleil perform this weekend at Harbourfront Centre as the Wonderful World of Circus, part of the Stella Artois World's Fare. Sufi singers from Pakistan and Rizwan- Muazzam Qawwali also perform, and there are fusion cooking courses, jazz and gypsy folk music. 235 Queen's Quay W. Visit harbourfrontcentre.com.
