The closing number is called You Can't Stop the Beat, and you wouldn't want to. This musical, about a plump teenage Baltimore girl who beats down doors to get crowned Miss Teenage Hairspray 1962 while also opening some for her black friends, stops being fun when it stops being fun; but that doesn't happen often. Sizzling choreography, a cleverly cartooned book, an infectious score, delightful performances by Jennifer Stewart and Tom Rooney. (Princess of Wales Theatre, 300 King St. W., 416-872-1212. Open run.)
