Elgin Theatre - 189 Yonge St. - 416-304-2901

Gershwin's masterpiece is performed, were told, "in the original Gullah dialect." Isnt it always? And do we need surtitles for some of the most famous songs of the 20th century? That's the insult; the injury comes in the performance itself, a much-toured production that starts with a sluggish orchestral prelude and never looks up. The staging is awful when static and worse when people move. Bess is just blowsy and Porgy most peculiar. Ends tomorrow.
