Song titles

Saturday, January 31, 1998

Toronto -- Reviewing a batch of books on Frank Sinatra (Jan. 10), I strongly criticized Michael Freedland's All the Way for its inaccuracies. Among other things, I accused Freedland of "inventing a song called If It's The Last Thing I Do ." In fact, that is the correct title of the Sammy Cahn-Saul Chaplin song. Freedland called it If It's The Last Thing You Do ;I, typing too hastily, inadvertently replaced his wrong title with the right one. So, if it's the last thing I do, I would like to set the record straight.
