Karl Valentin was a German comedian who flourished until the Second World War, which he more or less sat out. Peter Froehlich, an authoritative performer but not a great clown, recreates his routines, aided by the sympathetic Nicola Lipman as his performance partner, Liesl Karlstadt, and the musical Peter Tiefenbach. It's an interesting evening but it doesn't have much urgency. (Tarragon Theatre, 30 Bridgeman Ave., 416-531-1827. Runs until May 30.)
