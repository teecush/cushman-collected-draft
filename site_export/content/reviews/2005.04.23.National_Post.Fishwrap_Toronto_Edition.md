Tarragon Extra Space - 30 Bridgman Ave. - 416-531-1827

David Macfarlane's play, well-publicized as being semi- autobiographical, is a monologue for a journalist who loses his job, his bike, his home (to the renovators) and -- here fiction starts -- his health and his wife. The gripes about the newspaper business, and about the habits of builders, are amusing, but the play turns mawkish in its last stretch. John Jarvis's performance is physically and vocally acrobatic. Through May 1.
