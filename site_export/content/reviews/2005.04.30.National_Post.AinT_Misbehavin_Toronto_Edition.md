Bluma Appel - 27 Front. St. E. - 416-368-3110

This Fats Waller tribute remains what it always was, a nightclub act to a higher power. Jackie Richardson shines as a member of the group. The three other women don't have enough material to go around, but the two men are fully extended, literally so in the case of the uncannily loose-limbed David Lopez, who is slithy as a tove. (See Lewis Carroll.) A welcome chance to encounter real singing, real dancing and real music in a Toronto musical. Through June 25.
