Princess of Wales Theatre - 300 King. St. W. - 416-872-1212

The best of the Rice-Lloyd Webber shows. Tim Rice's political satire has more unforced edge than were used to in musicals, especially now. His more conventional lyrics are less impressive. Eva Perons story runs thin in the second act and the show has no characters; Eva (Kathy Voytko, hard as nails but charmless) is a symbol, Che (Bradley Dean, singing in a choppy falsetto) a commentator. Even when they sing together, they don't meet. Ends tomorrow.
