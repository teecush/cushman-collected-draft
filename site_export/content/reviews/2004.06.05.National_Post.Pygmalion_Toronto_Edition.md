Festival Theatre, Niagara-on-the-Lake, Ont., 1-800-511-7429. Runs in repertory until November.

A treat. Shaw's comedy, an effortlessly witty hard-edged romance, is given a panoramic production, splendiferously designed, with Jim Mezon and Tara Rosling charting a clear and exhilarating line through the relationship of the professor and the flower girl, and Simon Bradbury delivering dustman Doolittles prose arias with superb aplomb. My Fair Lady hasnt killed this play off; there's room for both, and one wouldn't want to be without either.
