Stephen Sondheim's Finishing the Hat surrounds his complete lyrics with commentary (on his own work and lyric-writing in general) thats as witty, graceful and perceptive as the songs themselves. In the same category of books even better than Id dared hope: Will Friedwald's Biographical Guide to the Great Jazz and Pop Singers offers essay-length appreciations of American Songbook performers, blessedly free of snobbery (though not of omissions). I'll be reading it well into 2011, and consulting it indefinitely thereafter.
