Neil Munro's tremendous produciton of this evolutionary sex comedy has Ben Carlson as its driving force, in a high-velocity performance that's a walking textbook on how to act GBS. Peter Hartwell's designs delight in playing games with the audience's expectations. The visual orchestration matches the verbal and makes it possible. Available in the three-act version; also with the Don Juan in Hell digression, with some of the best-turned orations Shaw ever dreamed up. (Festival Theatre, Niagara-on-the-Lake. 1-800-511- 7429. Until Oct. 9.)
