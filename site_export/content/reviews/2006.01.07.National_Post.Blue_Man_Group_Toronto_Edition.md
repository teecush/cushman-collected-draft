Panasonic Theatre - 651 Yonge St. - 416-872-1111

By turns charming, boring and annoying, the Blue Men specialize in percussion-pounding and juggling. A lot of candy-munching gets done, and the chomping, too, is rhythmic. They also project a remarkable amount of personality, considering that their faces are entirely covered in blue masks. They don't speak, but some of their best humour involves written words. Indefinite run.
