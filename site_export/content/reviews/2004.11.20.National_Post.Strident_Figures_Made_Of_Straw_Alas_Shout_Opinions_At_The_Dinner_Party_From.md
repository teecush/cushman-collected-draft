Omnium Gatherum is the dinner party from hell. In the wake of 9/ 11, seven or eight people (there's a surprise late arrival) gather for a meal and to shout their opinions at, or past, one another. The characters in this U.S. play are made of straw and the conversation, when not strident, can be bewilderingly trivial, if never actually boring. Some crude performances are offset by good ones from Nigel Shawn Williams and Fiona Reid. (Berkeley Street Theatre. Ends tonight. 416-368-3110.)
