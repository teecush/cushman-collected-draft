Second City's "55th Sketch Comedy Revue" has the series best title in recent memory but is the worst show. Loud and angry political satire is promised, but it boils down to anti-Bush jokes that are as lazy and repetitive as what theyre lampooning. The good stuff includes numbers about gay maternity leave, office psychobabble and Rochester ("the Winnipeg of America"). The performers, as usual, are fine; but they're also the writers. (Tim Sims Playhouse, 56 Blue Jays Way, 416-343-0011. Open run.)
