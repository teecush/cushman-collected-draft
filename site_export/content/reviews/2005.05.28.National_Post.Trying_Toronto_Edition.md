Berkeley Street Theatre - 27 Berkeley St. -416-368-3110

An autobiographical piece by Joanna McClelland Glass who seems, 40 years ago, to have been an exemplary secretary to Francis Biddle, former attorney-general and Nuremberg judge. She reminds him of his liberal past and introduces him to the Dictaphone. This cross- generational two-hander (Paul Soles crotchety without being obnoxiously endearing, Caroline Cave prone to attacks of poetry- voice) is painless but bland. Through June 11. R.C.

Christina Poddubiuk's study-set looks a beautiful place to live and think in. Through June 11.
