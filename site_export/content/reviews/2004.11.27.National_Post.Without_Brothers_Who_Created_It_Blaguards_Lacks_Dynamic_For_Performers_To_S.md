A Couple of Blaguards was conceived and originally performed by Frank McCourt (Angela's Ashes) and his brother Malachy. Done by other actors it's only mildly entertaining, since there are no real characterizations, and no dynamic, for the performers to seize upon. The first, better, half concerns growing up in the poorest part of Limerick and is full of easy, though still funny, jabs at Irish Catholicism. The second, after the brothers have emigrated to New York, is thoroughly arbitrary. (Winter Garden Theatre, until Dec. 12. 416-314-2901)
