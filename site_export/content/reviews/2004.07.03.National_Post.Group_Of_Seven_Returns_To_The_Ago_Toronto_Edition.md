Some 84 years after their first opening, the Group of Seven have returned to the AGO. A new installation features 50 paintings to add to the gallery's already impressive collection from Canada's pre- eminent artists. Expect swirling skies and trees from the hinterlands. Art Gallery of Ontario, 317 Dundas St. E.
