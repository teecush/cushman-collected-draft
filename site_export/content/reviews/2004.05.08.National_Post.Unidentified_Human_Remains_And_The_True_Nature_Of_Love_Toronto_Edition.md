A new production of the play that made Brad Fraser's name back in 1990, with a wit and theatrical presence that are still uncommon. The remains are those of the female victims of a serial killer at large in Edmonton, while love is sought in some form by each of the play's seven characters. Performances are mostly fine, with Damien Atkins impressive and enjoyable as David, who gets all the best lines and goes the longest journey. Buddies in Bad Times Theatre, 12 Alexander St., 416-975-8555. Runs until May 16.
