Dream Machine Theatre Passe Muraille - 16 Ryerson Ave. - 416-504- 7529

It makes you feel good, like a dream machine should. Calgary's One Yellow Rabbit troupe comes back to town with its revue-like tribute to Ginsberg, Burroughs and other Beats. Blake Brooker's lyrics are angular and stylish, David Rhymer's music pungent and jazzy. It's also well sung, while the visuals are superb. The show aims to represent a theatrical equivalent of a "drugless high" and - - though the spell wears off toward the end -- it succeeds. Through March 20.
