Factory Theatre - 125 Bathurst St. - 416-504-9971

Rick Miller's Bigger Than Jesus is a seriously comic investigation of Christianity. Parts of it are brilliant and others silly, but though the latter predominate, it's the former you'll remember. Miller's self-effacing vocal manner co-exists with absolute physical and technical command. The best sections include a slick, fast, witty lecture on the Gospels as history and a sequence when Miller turns into a cross between Jesus and a baffled comedian facing a tough house. Through Oct. 15.
