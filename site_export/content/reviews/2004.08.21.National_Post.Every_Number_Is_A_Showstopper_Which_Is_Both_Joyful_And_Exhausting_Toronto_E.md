The Cole Porter songs in Anything Goes are prime numbers, but they don't add up to make a story. All the same, this is a joyous example of the old, raffish metropolitan musical, with a good, screwball book, light and witty staging and good performances (though Cynthia Dale is no Ethel Merman). Every number's a showstopper, which is joyful if exhausting. (Avon Theatre, Stratford, Ontario. Runs until Oct. 31. 1-800-567-1600. stratfordfestival.com).
