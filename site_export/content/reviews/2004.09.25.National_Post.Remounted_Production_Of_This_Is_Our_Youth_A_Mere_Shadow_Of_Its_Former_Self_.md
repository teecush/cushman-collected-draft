If This is Our Youth, Kenneth Lonergan's autobiographical play, can survive this, it can survive anything. The bones of Woody Harrelson's formerly excellent production are still visible, but the flesh has decayed beyond recognition. Of the three actors playing young, spoiled drug-addled New Yorkers, two -- Katherine Isabelle and Jason (Sex and the City) Lewis -- have little idea of theatrical projection. Marcello Cabezas has some energy and focus but has become hysterical. (Berkeley Street Theatre. Until Oct. 2. 416-368- 3110.)
