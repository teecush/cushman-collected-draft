Theatre Centre - 1087 Queen St. W. - 416-504-7529

An imaginative, ingenious, multicultural and multi-ethnic staging, though Soheil Parsa's production does have a habit of undercutting its own best moments; a first-rate cauldron scene (minus the cauldron) is followed by a gratuitous war dance for Macbeth himself. Much of the show is about black scarves, for witches, and gold robes, for kings. It doesn't plot much of a course for its two leading characters, but it leads us excitingly into their world. Ends tomorrow.
