Shakespeares happy endings choke you up: Their family reunions have the power of myth. Thats how it works in this new production of Cymbeline, a fairy tale trying to pass itself off as a history play, crammed with story and with enjoyably shameless theatrical devices, plus the most exquisite dirge in the English language. Martha Henry is at her silkiest as the wicked Queen; Claire Jullien as Shakespeare's only married heroine acts with delicacy, strength and humour. (Tom Patterson Theatre, Stratford. Until Sept. 26. 1- 800-567-1600.)
