Tarragon Theatre - 30 Bridgman Ave. - 416-531-1827

Two couples -- one married, one engaged -- come to Las Vegas, though much of Morwyn Brebner's play might still have happened if they'd stayed in Canada. The characters are well-drawn, and well- acted, but their interaction seems random. A fine neat-closing speech accurately captures how most of us live our lives. Through Oct. 23.
