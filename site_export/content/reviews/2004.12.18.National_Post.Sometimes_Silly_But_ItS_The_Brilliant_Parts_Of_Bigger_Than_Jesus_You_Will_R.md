Rick Millers Bigger Than Jesus is a seriously comic investigation of Christianity. Parts of it are brilliant and other parts silly, but though the latter predominate, its the former you'll remember. Miller's self-effacing vocal manner coexists with absolute physical and technical command. The best sections include a slick, fast, witty lecture on the Gospels as history and a sequence when Miller turns into a cross between Jesus himself and a baffled comedian facing a tough house. (Factory Theatre, until Dec. 19. 416- 504-9971.)
