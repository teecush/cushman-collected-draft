Theatre Passe Muraille - 16 Ryerson Ave. - 416-504-7529

A danced duet, both verbal and physical, of approach and avoidance, consummation and revulsion. Ernest and Ernestine are a shy pair who get married. It's a given that these lovebirds, otherwise temperamental opposites, are going to end up hating one another without ever quite ceasing to love one another, but this 1987 piece makes it fresh and clever. Rick Roberts and Jenny Young meet its great technical demands with insouciant assurance. Ends tomorrow.
