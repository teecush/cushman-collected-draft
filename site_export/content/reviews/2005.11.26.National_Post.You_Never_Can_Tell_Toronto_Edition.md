Festival Theatre - Niagara-on-the-Lake - 1-800-511-7429

This is the Shaw comedy with the amorous dentist, the seaside hotel setting, the omniscient waiter and the precocious twins who are here brought sparklingly off by Harry Judge and Nicole Underhay. But Morris Panych's production sets off so many fireworks that the play's real ones are reduced to squibs. Still, you can't wholly resist a show in which the music of choice for a dentist's office is Fixing a Hole. Closes tonight.
