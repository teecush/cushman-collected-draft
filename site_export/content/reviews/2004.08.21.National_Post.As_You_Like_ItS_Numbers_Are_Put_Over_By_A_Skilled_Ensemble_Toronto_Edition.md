James Macdonald's lively and warm-hearted production, the best in High Park in several summers, takes many liberties but gets the essentials right. It's in modern dress, and much of the abundant music (the play as written is practically a musical) is doo-wop. The numbers are put over by a skilled ensemble, led by the lithe and warm-voiced Gavin Hope as Amiens. As Rosalind -- the official lead guy -- Alannah Harkin is a charmer. (Outdoor Amphitheatre in High Park, Bloor Street West and High Park Avenue. 416-367-1652. Runs until Sept. 5).
