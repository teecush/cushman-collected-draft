Some years ago, ace stage magician David Ben had a show at the Shaw Festival called The Conjuror, which qualified for that theatre's program by having Ben dress up in exotic Victorian gear and perform feats that were presumably taken from magic books of the period. He turned out to be a better magician than actor, but his tricks really are amazing, and more of them are on view for the holidays in a show that's called simply Tricks. (Artword Theatre, Ends Jan. 2. 416-870-8000).
