Tarragon Extra Space. - 30 Bridgman Ave. - 416-531-1827

In this outstanding new play, Rose Laborde's depiction of three young people growing up in Chile in the Allende years keeps shifting the ground, deftly and devastatingly, from the political to the personal and back again. Three fine performances (Salvatore Antonio, Cara Pifko, Sergio Di Zio) on a three-cornered set. Through March 18.
