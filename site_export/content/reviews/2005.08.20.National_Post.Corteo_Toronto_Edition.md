Ontario Place - 955 Lake Shore Blvd. W - 1-800-450-1480

Cirque du Soleil's latest has a theme -- a dying clown looks back on past circus experiences (a metaphor for life). The skills are awesome, but this edition falls short of its predecessors in beauty and daring. The most delightful sequence involves a very small adult floating out over the audience while attached to five balloons, while her daddy-clown asks for her to be given back. Through Sept. 11.
