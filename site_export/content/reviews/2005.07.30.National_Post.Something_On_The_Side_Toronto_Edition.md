Royal George Theatre - Niagara-on-the-Lake - 1-800-51d1-7429

The Epoque depicted in Georges Feydeau's farces seems more Belle to us than it probably did then, so we camp them up. Neil Munros lunchtime production has more than its share of moustache-twirling acting but its very entertaining, with excellent performances by Ric Reid as a complacent would-be lecher and Simon Bradbury as an all-knowing maitre d'. Sue LePage designs another fine eatery. In repertory through Sept. 25.
