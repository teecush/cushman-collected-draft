Coronation Park - Oakville - 905-815-4049

One of the best Shakespeares to come along indoors or out. Miles Potter's direction approaches text and actors with an open, alert mind and gets the best out of both. The lovers are recognizable teenagers, with their long four-sided quarrel very carefully built. Interplay among the Athenian amateur actors is equally rich. The fairies fare less well, with a resentful show-off Puck the best of them. Through August 6.
