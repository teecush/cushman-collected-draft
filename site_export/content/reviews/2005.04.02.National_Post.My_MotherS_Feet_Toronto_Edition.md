Berkeley Upstairs - 26 Berkeley St. - 416-368-3110

First-time playwright Gina Wilkinson has a striking way with words, one that compels attention without always rewarding it. Dans mom, for whom mothering means smothering, has lost her feet; Dan tells us how, at length and with much metaphor. Tom Rooney's narrator-protagonist is enchanting; Jerry Franken unassumingly versatile. Beautifully directed by Micheline Chevrier, who makes simplicity and concentration into a style. Ends tonight.
