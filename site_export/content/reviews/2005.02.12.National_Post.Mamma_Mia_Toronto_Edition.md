Royal Alexandra Theatre - 260 King St. W. - 416-872-1212

The score for Mamma Mia! is drawn from ABBA hits of the '70s, and in this production they are shoehorned into a story that in no way concerns them. Instead, the musical's script raises issues about marriage and parenthood, and ends in a lame reprise of most of ABBA's hits: a pop concert, in fact. But for nearly five years now, the audience has been greeting each song with a thunderous clap of recognition. Not only do they applaud; they laugh. Indefinite run.
