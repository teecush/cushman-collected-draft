This revival of one of Cirque du Soleil's greatest hits has some kind of story, but dont worry about it. Its about raising the bar, continually seeking a higher springboard. A fire-eater not only eats fire but juggles with it; there are contortionists apparently without anything as inflexible as a bone in their bodies; there's a magical snowstorm; a man walking on his hands proves it's the Olympic people who should have scouted the Cirque for talent, rather than the other way around. (Ontario Place, 800-361-4595. Ends tomorrow.)
