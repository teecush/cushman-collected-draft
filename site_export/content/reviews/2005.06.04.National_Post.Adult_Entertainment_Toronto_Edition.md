Factory Theatre - 125 Bathurst St. - 416-504-9971

Taut, funny, mordant, surprising, full of sex and violence and a bit thin. George F. Walker's play (one-sixth of his Suburban Motel suite) provides a concentrated portrait of four souls in purgatory: two bent cops, a lawyer with an angle, and one comparative innocent (who learns that tar will defile you anyway). The performers are fine, but they hold us as actors rather than as people. The language doesn't sing but it snaps. Through June 12.
