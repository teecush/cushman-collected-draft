Theatre Centre - 1087 Queen St. W. - 416-534-9261

In this speech-and-dance piece, sundry people with suitcases in a plush hotel lobby behave as if they think they're in Shakespeare. William Webster reprises his Polonius, previews his King Lear (in the most substantial sequence) and gets a superbly contemptuous crack at Richard III. Choreographer Claudia Moore's Lady Macbeth suggests a second career. Closes tomorrow.
