Berkeley Downstairs - 26 Berkeley St. - 416-368-3110

A "clown play," licensing its performers to be grotesque while mostly absolving them from being funny. Creator-director Michael Kennard presents a power struggle between Clowns, who have red noses and sit on thrones, and Bouffons, who are kept in dungeons from which they emerge, bent and cringing, and are commanded to entertain. Their routines almost save the show's first half. The second sinks in clumsy political satire, but with a sophisticated twist. Through April 9.
