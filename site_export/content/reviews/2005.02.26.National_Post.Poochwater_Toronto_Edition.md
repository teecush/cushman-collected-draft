Theatre Passe Muraille - 16 Ryerson Ave. - 416-504-7529

Though it comes in from the Fringe bearing Dora awards and four- star reviews, Poochwater is a mangy dog. In this two-hander, a man (played by author Mike McPhaden) loses his memory and identity. The moment of realization is stomach-churningly powerful, and there's also a sweet sequence in which the characters, both ex-GIs, converse in the darkness in Morse code. Most of the writing, though, is irrepressibly cute and the performances loud. Ends tomorrow.
