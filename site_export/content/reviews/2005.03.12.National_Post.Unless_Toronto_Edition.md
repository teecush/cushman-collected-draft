Bluma Appel Theatre - 27 Front St. E. - 416-368-3110

Most of what's on stage in Unless seems to be there purely because it was once on the page somewhere else -- in the late Carol Shields's novel, adapted by Shield and her daughter Sara Cassidy. It is, fittingly enough, about a mother -- a writer of "light fiction" - - and her daughter, who suddenly drops out and joins the homeless. The devastated mother spends most of the time addressing us directly; her scenes with other people are just sketches. Through March 19.
