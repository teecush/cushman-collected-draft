Ginger 2 (Upstairs) - 403 Yonge St. - 416-831-0543

A site-specific production, the site being a squatters' loft at Yonge and Gerrard, standing in for a Poverty Row room in New York, inhabited by a couple of junkies. Hes an incontinent Gulf War veteran, shes an ex-stripper with hepatitis. He attempts to haul her out of it, and we find ourselves pulling for them. The production spares us little, and the play risks, though mostly skirts, sentimentality. It's a compelling evening -- in this specialized environment, a riveting one.
