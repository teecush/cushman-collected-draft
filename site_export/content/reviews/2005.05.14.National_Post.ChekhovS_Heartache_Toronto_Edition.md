Factory Studio - 125 Bathurst St. - 416-504-9971

The fourth of Theatre Smith Gilmours dramatizations of Chekhovs short stories. Peasants and Peasant Women, two of the bleakest, here appear bleaker still, since the originals' swift narrative movement turns into a trudge on stage. Claire Calman and Monica Dottor are fine, but Michele Smith and Dean Gilmour are problematic: she unable to give sense and flavour to English speech and he lacking range for the acting challenges he sets himself. Through May 29.
