Massey Hall - 178 Victoria St. - 416-872-4255

Louise Pitre conquers, as both singer and actress, in a semi- concert version of Irving Berlin's musical: a show with a great collection of songs, if not quite a great score (or a great book; there's virtually no second act). Billy Ray Cyrus acts sweetly but hasn't the voice for his songs; sure, anything he can do she can do better, but it shouldn't be quite such a foregone conclusion. Ends tonight.
