Tarragon Theatre - 30 Bridgman St. - 416-531-1827

Susan Coynes latest play is partly about a middle-aged woman trapped in an apparently constricting (offstage) marriage, partly about the various relationships between literature and life, teachers and students. Other elements include psychics and college life and the ghostly presences of Harry Houdini and William James, but they don't add up. There are some very funny jokes, both verbal and situational, and a toweringly delightful turn by Nancy Palk. Through May 29. R.C.

Not dull, but not satisfying either. Through May 29.
