Artword Theatre - 75 Portland St. - 416-408-1146

John Van Burek translated, directed, produced and (in a small way) acts in the English-language premiere of an extraordinary transitional comedy by 18th-century master craftsman Carlo Goldoni. Fine performances by Christine Brubaker and Jerry Franken, whose last-act duet puts the show unstoppably on course. Closes tomorrow.
