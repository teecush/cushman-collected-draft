Theatre Passe Murraille Backspace - 16 Ryerson Ave. - 416-504- 8980

The program announces A Play about the Religious Right. Sex. Violence. Tom Walmsley's three-hander portrays its two married evangelical Christians as sexual hypocrites, which isn't too interesting dramatically. But the sex and violence are good. Witty performances by Jordan Pettle, David Jansen and Patricia Fagan. Through March 12.
