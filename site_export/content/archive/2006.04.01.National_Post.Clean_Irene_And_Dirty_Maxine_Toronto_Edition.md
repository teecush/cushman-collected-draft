Buddies in Bad Times Theatre - 12 Alexander St. - 416-975-8555

Anna Chatterton and Evalyn Parry offer a rhymed cautionary alphabet: contemporary urban females (from Arty Marty to Yoga Lola) who come to bad ends. They use their voices percussively and their bodies pneumatically, but their physical wit is not matched by their verbal. Closes tomorrow.
