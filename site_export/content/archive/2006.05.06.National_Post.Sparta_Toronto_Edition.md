THEATRE PASSE MURAILLE

16 Ryerson Ave. - 416-504-7529

Back to the farm. Sarah Martyn's play has a good sense of family tensions, and of economic-agricultural realities, but no theme, story or believable characters to hitch them to. Layne Coleman's direction, on an awkwardly semi-stylized set, is of little help to either the play or the actors. Closes tomorrow.
