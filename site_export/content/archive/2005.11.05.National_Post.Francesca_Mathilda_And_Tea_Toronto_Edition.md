Theatre Passe Muraille - 16 Ryerson Ave. - 416-504-8980

Anna Chatterton and Evalyn Parry (the Mysterious Aunties) are better than their material, which they write themselves. The first half recycles received ideas about Victorian womanhood; the second, titled The Mysterious Shorts, takes us on a revue sketch tour from the 1920s to the '80s. Best things: Parry as Rosalind Russell being hard-boiled, and both parodying breathless teen fiction. Closes tomorrow.
