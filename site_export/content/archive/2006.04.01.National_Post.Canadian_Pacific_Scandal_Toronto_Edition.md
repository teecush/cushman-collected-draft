Cameron House - 408 Queen St. W. - 416-703-0811

VideoCabaret tells us, again, that politicians are egomaniacal crooks. Part 6 of this comic-strip history deals with the Caledonian Age of Canadian politics: Macdonald vs. Mackenzie, colourful carpetbagger and prohibitionsit prig. Presentation is still dazzling, but one ceases to care who is screwing whom. Limited run.
