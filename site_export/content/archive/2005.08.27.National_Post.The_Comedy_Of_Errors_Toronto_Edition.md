Theatre by the Bay - Heritage Park, Barrie - 1-866-735-9243

In Richard Rose's production of Shakespeares farce, Ephesus is a town where everyone lives in transparent cages. The idea, oppressive to begin with, gains traction as the show proceeds; by the close its very funny, with a surprising amount of human depth and a surprise ending that's enchantingly right for a play about twins The production ends its run next weekend, so you're advised to see it now. Through Sept. 3.
