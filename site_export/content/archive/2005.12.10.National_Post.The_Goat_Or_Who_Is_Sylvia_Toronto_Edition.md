Berkeley Street Theatre - 26 Berkeley St. - 416-368-3110

That's easy: Sylvia is a goat, the, with whom a successful and previously happily married architect falls in love. This production of Edward Albee's latest anatomy of marriage (and one of his wittiest plays) is distinguished by R.H. Thomson's delicate worried performance and Judith Bowden's set. Closes tonight.
