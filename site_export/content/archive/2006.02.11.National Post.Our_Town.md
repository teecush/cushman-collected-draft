Soulpepper's first show in their new theatre, and perfect. Joseph Ziegler's production of Thornton Wilders ambiguous celebration of small-town life is a model of tact and control, as is Albert Schultzs narrating Stage Manager. Martha MacIsaac's earnest Emily makes the last act devastating. In repertory through March 25. R.C.
