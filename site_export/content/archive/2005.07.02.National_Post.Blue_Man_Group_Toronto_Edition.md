By turns charming, boring and annoying, the Blue Men specialize in percussion and juggling, with marshmallows and gumballs among other things. A lot of candy-munching gets done, and the chomping is rhythmic. They also project a remarkable amount of personality, considering that their faces are covered in blue masks. They don't speak, but some of their best humour involves written words. A high- tech show that debunks technology; some call that postmodern. Indefinite run.
