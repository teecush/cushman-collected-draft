Artword Theatre - 75 Portland St. - 416-408-1146

This dramatization of Dostoevsky's novella splits the playing time and the stage space between the story itself and the equally dramatic way it came to be written. Unfortunately, the two parts are never brought together; the doggedly literal adaptation is writing- by-numbers and some of the actors suffer from double-trouble. Closes tomorrow.
