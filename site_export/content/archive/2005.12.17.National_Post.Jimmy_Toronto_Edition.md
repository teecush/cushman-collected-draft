Buddies in Bad Times - 12 Alexander St. - 416-975-8555

Marie Brassard's self-performed solo play concerns a self- described "homosexual hairdresser" who was the subject of two people's dreams, 50 years apart. This whimsical device is the pretext for a play about love, sometimes poignant, often sentimental; the performance is on a higher plane (literally) than the text. Closes tomorrow.
