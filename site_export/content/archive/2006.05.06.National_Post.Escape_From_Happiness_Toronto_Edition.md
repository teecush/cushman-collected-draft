FACTORY THEATRE

125 Bathurst St. - 416-504-9971

George F. Walker's 1992 play is funny, a bit suspenseful and generally more fun than most, with a tough mouth and a soft heart. It also has two separate plots -- family and crime -- that don't quite cohere. Irene Poole, in one of her killer-dog roles, gives a tornado of a performance, and Clare Coulter is triumphant as a kind of secular saint. Closes tomorrow.
