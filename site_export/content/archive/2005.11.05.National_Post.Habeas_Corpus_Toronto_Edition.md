Bluma Appel - 27 Front St. E. - 416-366-7723

Alan Bennetts 1973 hit is a by-now-nostalgic look at what its characters repeatedly call, in varying shades of delight and dismay, the Permissive Society. These days it seems a long way from home, in both time and space, and Morris Panych's unrelentingly open-plan production, denying realism, also denies farce. Joseph Ziegler and Fiona Reid are good; Melody Johnson is a treat. Closes tonight.
