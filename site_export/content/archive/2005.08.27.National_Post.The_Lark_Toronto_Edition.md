Festival Theatre - Stratford - 1-800-567-1600

Jean Anouilh's play makes no effort to explain Joan of Arc, and in Amanda Plummers performance she remains a mystery, though a haunting one. Michael Lindsay-Hoggs production is fine and rigorous, though not quite right for Anouilh's historical fairy tale. It's updated to Vichy France, with Graham Abbey a Nazi Warwick, Martha Henry a very Spanish Inquisitor and Steven Sutcliffe a brilliant Dauphin, In repertory through Oct. 29.
