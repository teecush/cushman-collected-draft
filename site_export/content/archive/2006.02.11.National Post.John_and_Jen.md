A two-hander American musical about, first, a brother and sister, and then, a mother and son. It ought to be terrible -- schematic and sentimental -- but isn't. It's honest, moving and occasionally witty. Real star-quality performances, both musically and dramatically, by Kyle Blair and Stephanie Roth. Closes tonight.
