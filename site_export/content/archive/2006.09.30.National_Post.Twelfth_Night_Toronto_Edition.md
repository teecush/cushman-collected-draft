Festival Theatre - Stratford 1-800-567-1600

The latest instalment in Leon Rubin's project of setting Shakespeare in exotic, usually Asian, climes. This time it's British Raj India, which does nothing for the play's non-existent political content, but sharpens relationships within Olivia's household. Seana McKenna is the lady, witty and alert as only a mature actress can be; Brian Bedford repeats and enhances his virtuoso Malvolio; Dana Green is an excellent Viola; and the Toby-Andrew-Maria nexus is unusually detailed. An intended dark ending doesn't come off. In rep through Oct. 28.
