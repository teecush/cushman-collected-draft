Berkeley Street Theatre - 26 Berkeley St. - 416-368-3110

In Ronnie Burkett's new puppet-play, a mother's love is felt, overwhelmingly, through its forcible removal. Darrell, a 50-year- old mentally challenged man, is orphaned but doesn't know it. The effect is heartbreaking, though the supplementary material, despite Burkett's technical virtuosity, feels like padding. Through June 24.
