Factory Theatre - 125 Bathurst St. - 416-504-9971

In this ponderously whimsical, would-be universal piece by Wajdi Mouawad (French-Canadian-Lebanese), a young man tries to bury his dead father, first at home, then in an unnamed country with rocky terrain and symbolic inhabitants. Father remains vocal throughout. Act I is tolerable, Acts II and III interminable. Performances aren't up to it. Through Dec. 11.
