Canon Theatre - 244 Victoria St. -416-872-1212

By current standards an unusually good show, though Twyla Tharp's choreography and the Billy Joel songs aren't always in perfect sync, while the characters don't have much character and the story is sappy. At its best, the show has the same kind of excitement as the great dance musicals West Side Story and A Chorus Line. But its form prevents it from taking that extra step. Through Dec. 31.
