Factory Theatre - 125 Bathurst St. - 416-504-9971

Another case of writing-by-numbers. Andrew Moodie's play depicts a black genius engineer in 19th-century America (both he and his greatest invention were the Real McCoy); it moves fast because there's nothing it wants to linger on. Maurice Dean Wint makes the protagonist a courteous man conversing in polysyllables. Through Feb. 26.
