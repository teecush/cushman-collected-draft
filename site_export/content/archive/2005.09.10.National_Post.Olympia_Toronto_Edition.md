Premiere Dance Theatre - 235 Queens Quay W. - 416-973-4000

A seeming trifle that develops teeth, in which imperial Austrians despise Hungarians (such as the play's author, Ferenc Molnar) and the latter avenge themselves. Albert Schultz's Soulpepper production could use more bite, but it looks elegant and features riotous performances from Nancy Palk and Oliver Dennis. As Olympia's mama, Palk makes Lady Bracknell seem like one of nature's democrats. In repertory through Sept. 19.
