MYSTERIOUSLY YOURS

2026 Yonge St. - 416-486-7469

The corpse in the latest interactive comedy-thriller from Mysteriously Yours dinner theatre is billionaire William Randolph, but he isn't a spoof Hearst or even a spoof Citizen Kane. The show is coasting, but Jean Daigle (as a Newfie cop) has as much zest ever. Indefinite run.
