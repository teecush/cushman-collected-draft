Berkeley Downstairs - 26 Berkeley St. - 416-368-3110

Richard Greenblatt's one-man show is about What Tom Lehrer Means to Him: a blend of recital, commentary and spiritual autobiography. The songs can't bear the dramatic weight he puts on them, and his singing lacks bite, but the show lights up when he speaks, all sabre- toothed self-deprecation, in his master's voice. Through Feb. 25.
