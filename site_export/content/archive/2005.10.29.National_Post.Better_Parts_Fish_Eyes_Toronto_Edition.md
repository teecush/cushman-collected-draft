Passe Muraille Backspace - 16 Ryerson Ave. - 416-504-8980A pair of monologues, performed by their authors. Nicole Stamp's Better Parts is a total joy, an actresss rhyming fantasies of being rich and famous, spoken to music: rap, but better. Anita Majumdars Fish Eyes, about a girl who doesn't want to go to India for a dance competition, is dexterously performed but lacks layers. Through Oct. 29 and Nov. 5 respectively.Word count: 83
