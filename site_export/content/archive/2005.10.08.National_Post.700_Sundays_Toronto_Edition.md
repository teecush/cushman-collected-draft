Canon Theatre - 244 Victoria St. - 416-877-1212

Billy Crystal's childhood environment was jazz and Jewish: a rich mix that makes for a fine, fun show, an autobiographical monologue that's a cross between a play and an especially elaborate stand-up routine. A scene involving an aunt whose daughter has had "a Lesbyterian wedding" is the funniest thing in ages. Closes tomorrow.
