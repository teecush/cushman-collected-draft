Court House Theatre - Niagara-on-the-Lake - 1-800-511-7429

Set in a big old Scottish house at the end of the 19th century, Ann-Marie MacDonald's "natural history" has action, farcical or melodramatic; much discussion, about evolution, kindness to animals, the implications of the word "hysteria"; and a web of literary allusions. But it's all weightless, though given a heavy-handed production. In repertory through Oct. 7.
