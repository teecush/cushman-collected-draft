Capitol Event Theatre

2492 Yonge St. - 416-322-0042

It's unbelievably bad. Four female stereotypes bob aimlessly and endlessly from floor to floor of Bloomingdales while discussing, or anyway mentioning, the change. They also sing forced and feeble new lyrics to 60s and '70s hits. After an hour-and-a-half of kvetching, they go affirmative on us, because one of them has discovered vibrators. The performers are thoroughly professional, but still. Indefinite run.
