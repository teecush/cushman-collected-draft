Berkeley Upstairs - 26 Berkeley St. - 416-368-3110

Caryl Churchill does A Number, a very good one, on cloning. There's a father, the unsociable son he traded in for an identical but more agreeable model, and one of the innumerable others whom the scientists ran up as bonuses. Fifty-five packed minutes of cat-and- mouse, with Shawn Doyle doing a fine job on all three of the issue-with-issues. Through Feb. 11.
