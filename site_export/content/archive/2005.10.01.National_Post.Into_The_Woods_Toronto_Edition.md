Avon Theatre - Stratford -1-800-567-1600

The Stephen Sondheim-James Lapine musical, moving and hilarious, is a cheerful cautionary tale with a darkness to it, and the combination, increasingly complex, makes a masterpiece. The show's greatest joy is the way in which it juggles and combines its mutiple fairy-tale sources; the musical and verbal themes are tightly and scintillatingly interwoven. Peter Hinton's production is visually provocative and dramatically lucid. In repertory through Oct. 30.
