Passe Muraille Backspace - 16 Ryerson Ave. -416-504-8980

Raoul Bhaneja's one-man Hamlet is a vanity project that sells the actor as well as the audience short. Bhaneja might make an interesting Hamlet, with a compelling line in instant hysteria, but not when he has to keep turning into other people. Visually he manages the instant changes very well, but he runs out of voices. Through Jan. 21.
