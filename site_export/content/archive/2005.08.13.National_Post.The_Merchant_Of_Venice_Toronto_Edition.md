Shakespeare in the Rough - Withrow Park - 647-438-6742

Sanjay Talwar's production offers gender-blind casting -- Shylock as a woman -- which also means tone-deaf casting; Shylock's famous line about his stolen turquoise becomes "I had it of Nathan when I was a young woman," which doesn't have the same, so to speak, ring. Otherwise the show is lively and intelligent, with some sharp editing and an excellent use of Withrow Park. Carly Street is an exceptional Portia. Through Sept. 5.
