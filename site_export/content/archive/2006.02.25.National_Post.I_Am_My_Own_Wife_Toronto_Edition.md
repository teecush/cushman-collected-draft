Bluma Appeal Theatre - 27 Front St. E. - 416-366-7723

Coming from New York loaded with awards, this turns out to be a very dull play. The story of Charlotte von Mahlsdorf, a German transvestite who survived the Nazis and Communists, is wrecked by the decision of the author to make it a play about himself as well. Stephen Ouimette plays Charlotte with delicacy and dignity. Through March 4.
