TARRAGON THEATRE

30 Bridgman Ave. - 416-531-1827

A clear example of the theatrical gulf between accuracy and truth. Diane Flacks and Richard Greenblatt play a mature couple with their first baby, plus assorted friends, helpers, lovers and -- the most compelling character -- the wife's dying father. The issues are compelling and a few of the jokes good, but the effect is sketchy. Through May 28.
