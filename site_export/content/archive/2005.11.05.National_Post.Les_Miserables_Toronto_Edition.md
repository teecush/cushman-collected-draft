Canon Theatre - 244 Victoria St. - 416-872-1212

"Jean Valjean, no evil-doer/ Stole some bread 'cause he was po- er/A detective chased him through a sewer/The End." Many prefer that Comden & Green precis of Les Mis to the later, longer Claude-Michel Schoenberg musical -- whose admirers can nevertheless catch it again, in all its grandiose banality, on what we are promised is its last tour. But I wouldn't count on it. Closes tonight.
