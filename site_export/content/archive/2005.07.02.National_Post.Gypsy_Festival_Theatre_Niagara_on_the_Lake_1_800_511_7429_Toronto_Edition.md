Gypsy has really only four characters, but they're large and so is the canvas on which they're presented. This is one show that needs to be done big, and in Jackie Maxwell's production it is. Above all, it's a triumph for Nora McLellan as Rose, the showbiz mother who pursues her daughter's success as if it were her own. She acts the part as a woman whose consuming need for love is only equalled by her inability to handle it. Through Oct. 30.
