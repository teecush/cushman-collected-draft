Princess of Wales Theatre - 300 King St. W. - 416-872-1212

Originally a homey, rambling comedy-drama about a Toronto hairdressing salon, Trey Anthony's play has been turned into a musical: a good idea but not carried far enough. The numbers are good but too few. Between them, a series of monologues celebrate the joys of life and chronicle the odds against them. All the confessors are well played: d.bi young -- plaintive, harrowing, hopeful -- is extraordinary. Through April 24.
