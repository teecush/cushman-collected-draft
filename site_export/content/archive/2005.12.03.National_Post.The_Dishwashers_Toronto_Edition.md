Tarragon Theatre - 30 Bridgman Ave. - 416-531-1827

On a startlingly detailed set by Ken MacDonald that takes us into the bowels of a vauntedly classy restaurant, three wage slaves (Randy Hughson, Eric Peterson, Jonathan Crombie) revolve in their predetermined patterns. Morris Panych's play has an idea, an environment and some really funny lines, but no development. Through Dec. 18.
