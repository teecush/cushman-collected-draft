Berkeley Street Theatre - 26 Berkeley St. - 416-368-3110

Apparently on her deathbed, Eleanor, combative widow of one medieval English king and mother of two others, states her case with no opposing voices. This is a miserable way of doing business dramatically and it short-changes history as well. Eleanor imagines herself arraigned for heresy, on grounds that might have surprised the Middle Ages. Catherine Muschamp's one-woman play is performed by Chapelle Jaffe, confident but monotonous. Closes tonight.
