The sixth instalment of the Walt Wingfield saga might also be called Wingfield's Insurance. The rural township's Orange Lodge burns down and stockbroker-turned-farmer Walt is put in charge of its reconstruction. Anecdotes, apparently self-contained and mostly farcical, come together at the end, like a team of shaggy-dog stories on a single leash. Rod Beattie, playing everybody, is, if you'll pardon the oxymoron, reliably amazing. Through Aug. 14.
