Bluma Appel Theatre - 27 Front St. E. - 416-366-7723

The singing's screechy, and most of the acting characterless, but much of the fault in this revival lies in the material; the songs tick off the prescribed '60s hippie attitudes like items in a catalogue. Outsiders are treated as a joke; insiders -- the Tribe -- with stultifying solemnity, so the Vietnam-draft conflict isn't very exciting. Through April 22.
