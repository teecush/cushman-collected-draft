Tarragon Theatre - 30 Bridgman Ave. - 416-531-1827

Charlotte Jones's play, clever and charming if inconclusive, concerns bees and roses and the plot of Hamlet, especially the relationship between the prince (now an English theoretical physicist) and his mother. In a good cast, these are excellently played by Fiona Reid and Dean Paul Gibson, though the latter never establishes himself at the centre of things. Through Feb. 12.
