Tarragon Extra Space - 30 Bridgman Ave. - 416-531-1827

Enjoyable and disappointing in equal measure, this is a Canadian- Bosnian co-creation (three of our actors, two of theirs, all good) in which a young man who got out of Sarajevo returns, with his new Canadian wife, and finds his family don't want his favours. The domestic issues swamp the political ones. Closes tomorrow.
