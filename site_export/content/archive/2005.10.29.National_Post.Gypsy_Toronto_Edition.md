Festival Theatre - Niagara-on-the-Lake - 1-800-511-7429

Gypsy has really only four characters, but they're large and so is the canvas on which theyre presented. This is one show that needs to be done big, and in Jackie Maxwells production it is. Above all, it's a triumph for Nora McLellan as Rose, the showbiz mother whose consuming need for love is equalled only by her inability to handle it. Ends tomorrow.
