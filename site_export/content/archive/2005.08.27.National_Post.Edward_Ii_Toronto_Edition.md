Studio Theatre - Stratford - 1-800-567-1600

Stratford's first production of a play by Marlowe, a study of powerlessness by a writer cruelly obsessed with power. In Richard Monette's production, clear and fast apart from its slow-motion battles, David Snelgrove is the doomed gay king, Michelle Giroux his two-faced masochistic queen, Jamie Robinson his other queen and Scott Wentworth the ruthless aspiring Mortimer. In repertory through Sept. 24.
