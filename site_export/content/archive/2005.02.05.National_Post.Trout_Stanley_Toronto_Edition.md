Claudia Dey, whose craft keeps getting better, delivers a masterwork. Glamorous Grace and quietly desperate Sugar, 30-year- old twin sisters, run the town garbage dump and manufacture tragic figurines respectively. This, without a hint of camp, is a play about people who live at extremes of climate and feeling, joyously written with tense, witty verbal duels and soaring prose arias. Directed by Eda Holmes, and unimprovably acted by Melody A. Johnson, Michelle Giroux and Gord Rand as the titular intruder, who is not a fish. Until Feb. 8. R.C.
