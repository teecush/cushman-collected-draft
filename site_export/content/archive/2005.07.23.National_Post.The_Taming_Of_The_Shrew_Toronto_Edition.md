ShakesepeareWorks - Ashbridge's Bay - 416-872-1111

Another Shakespeare comedy set in Italy, this time updated to 1913. David Ferry's production is rough in its opening stretches but gets interesting, with a Petruchio (Paulino Nunes) and Katharina (Elizabeth Saunders) who take off after the intermission. There are some good comedians on board as well, among them Christopher Morris (Tranio) and Dylan Roberts (Biondello), and the last scene is splendidly staged. Through Aug. 7.
