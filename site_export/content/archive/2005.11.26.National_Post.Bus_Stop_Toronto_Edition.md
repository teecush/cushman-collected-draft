Royal George Theatre - Niagara-on-the-Lake - 1-800-511-7429

Of its kind, as good as it gets. William Inge's 1955 Broadway hit calls up memories of a tidier theatrical time. As the naive cowboy and the floosie-chantoosie, Martin Happer and Nicole Underhay bring two American archetypes to individual life, and the expected happy ending has its dark underside. The roadside diner designed by Sue LePage looks good enough to be franchised. Closes tomorrow.
