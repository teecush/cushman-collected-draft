Sam Shepard's best play in an exciting production, with Stuart Hughes and Megan Follows alternately scary and farcically scared as Eddie and May, feuding but unbreakable lovers caught in an anachronistic dream of the Old West, represented by an Old Man who sits non-naturalistically at the side of the stage and knows all about both of them. In repertory through Oct. 1.
