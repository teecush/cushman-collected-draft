Young Centre - Distillery District - 416-866-8666

Beast or nickel, the American buffalo is an endangered species. Stuart Hughes' Soulpepper production is lucid and entertaining, but lacks the terror and exuberance proper to David Mamet's backstreet Chicago hustlers. Ted Dykstra has the soul of the paranoid Teach, but not his excess; Michael Hanrahan's Donny is understated and compassionate. Through April 29.
