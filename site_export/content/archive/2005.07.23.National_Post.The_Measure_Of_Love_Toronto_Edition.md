Studio Theatre - Stratford - 1-800-567-1600

The first half of this double-bill is a new play by Nicolas Billon with more contrivance than substance, but touching in some of its revelations about two former convent schoolmates (Fiona Reid and Diana Leblanc). The second half presents Lally Cadeau in a selection of monologues by the legendary Ruth Draper. Draper, a genius, wrote the pieces for herself and there's no point in varying a single pause or inflection, so the actress's task is hopeless. Through Sept. 25.
