Bluma Appel Theatre - 27 Front St. E. - 416-366-7723

Joan MacLeod's play is a domestic drama with a supposed larger historical theme: The abuse of British children dispatched to Canada 100 years ago. But it takes a long time to get to the point, and an elaborate rocky set is unfriendly. Eric Peterson (as an invalid, again) eventually and thrillingly explodes with shame and anger. Through Jan. 28.
