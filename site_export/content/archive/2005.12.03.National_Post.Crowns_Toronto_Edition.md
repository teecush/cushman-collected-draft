Bluma Appel Theatre - 27 Front St. E. - 416-366-7723

All about hats, gorgeous headgear worn by women in the segregated South to keep up morale and appearances. An animated scrapbook: disconnected monologues, paralyzingly dull, interspersed with gospel, Jackie Richardson in great voice. The audience is dragooned into clapping along, to persuade itself it's having a good time. Through Dec. 10.
