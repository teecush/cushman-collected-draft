Theatre Centre - 1087 Queen St. W. *416-538-0988

Mostly talk, but very good talk. Anosh Irani's title refers to a cheap drug but is also a fair description of the desperation of the three characters: an exotic dancer, her mother/manager, a blind client. A story of love and revenge unfolds, pungently and lyrically. Anita Majumdar's dancer is very good at her job. Closes tomorrow.
