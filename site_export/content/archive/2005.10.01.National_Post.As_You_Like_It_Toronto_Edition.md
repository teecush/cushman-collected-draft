Festival Theatre - Stratford -1-800-567-1600

A good conventional production, with agreeable new music by Barenaked Ladies and set in the 1960s "summer of love," which was a lot more soft-centred than the play is. Antoni Cimolino's production is best when it takes itself and its author seriously. Sara Tophams Rosalind doesnt fill the space, but there's good supporting work from Dion Johnstone, Stephen Ouimette, Sophie Goulet and Graham Abbey. In repertory through Oct. 30.
