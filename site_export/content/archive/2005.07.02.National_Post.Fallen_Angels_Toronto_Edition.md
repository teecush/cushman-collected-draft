In Noel Coward's 1924 comedy, two rich married ladies await the arrival of an ex-lover of both, fortifying themselves with food, much drink and outright violence, while their off-putting husbands are off putting. The rudeness still has a kick. Brian Bedford's production combines verbal precision with physical madness. Seana McKenna and Lucy Peacock (plus Joyce Campion as a Jeevesian maid) are Absolutely Fabulous. Through Oct. 29.
