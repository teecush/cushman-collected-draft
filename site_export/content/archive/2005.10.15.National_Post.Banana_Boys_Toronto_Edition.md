Factory Studio - 125 Bathurst St. - 416-504-9971. 5. R.C.

In Leon Aureus's play (from Terry Woo's novel), a high-energy acting quintet portray five second-generation Chinese-Canadians -- "yellow outside, white within" -- growing up together, and then apart. The group scenes are rambunctiously enjoyable, even when the mood is desperate, but the two central figures are dramatic stereotypes so the action winds down. Closes tomorrow.
