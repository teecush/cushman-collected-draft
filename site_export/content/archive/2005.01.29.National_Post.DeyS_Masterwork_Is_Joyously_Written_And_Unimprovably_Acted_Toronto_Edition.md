In Trout Stanley, Claudia Dey delivers a masterwork. Grace and Sugar, 30-year-old twins, run the town garbage dump and manufacture tragic figurines, respectively. This, without a hit of camp, is a play about people who live at extremes of climate and feeling, joyously written with tense, witty verbal duels and soaring prose arias. With Melody A. Johnson, Michelle Giroux and Gord Rand. (Factory Theatre, until Feb. 8, 416-504-9971)
