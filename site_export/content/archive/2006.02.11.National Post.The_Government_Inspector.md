A hit-and-miss production in which the hits are ecstatic, especially Diego Matamoros's great farcical performance as the penniless man mistaken for an investigating official. Morris Panych's adaptation goes, self-defeatingly, for conscious theatricality, but his staging is, on its own hectic terms, excellent. In repertory through March 23.
