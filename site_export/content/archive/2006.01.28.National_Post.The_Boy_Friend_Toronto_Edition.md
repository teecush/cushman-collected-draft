Royal Alexandra Theatre - 260 King St. W. - 416-872-1212

Sandy Wilson's "new musical comedy of the 1920s" can be both intoxicating and enchanting. In Julie Andrews's production you settle for intoxication, with special credit to John DeLuca's charleston choreography, Andrea Chamberlain's soubrette and Tony Walton's Riviera-by-night setting. Through Feb. 18.
