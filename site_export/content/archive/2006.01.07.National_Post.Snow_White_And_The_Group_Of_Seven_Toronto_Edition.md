Elgin Theatre - 189 Yonge St. - 416-597-0965

They aren't painters, they're a pop group, and most of them are ex-Canadian Idols, as are the spunky Snow White (Elena Juatco) and her dimwit Prince (Jamie McKnight). Ross Petty's latest panto, in which he's both villain and Dame, breaks new ground with a script that includes exposition and deconstruction, and music that's part of the plot. Ends tomorrow.
