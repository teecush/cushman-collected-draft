Tom Patterson Theatre - Stratford - 1-800-567-1600

The ultimate Tennessee Williams play -- set in the Deepest South and by turns hypnotic, ludicrous and both at once -- is given an unmissable production by Miles Potter. Especially superb are the duets between Seana McKenna's Italian storekeeper, consumed by a dry and bitter fire, and Jonathan Goad's itinerant musician and reformed stud. In repertory through Sept. 25.
