Welcome to Cushman Collected, the collected reviews of internationally acclaimed and award-winning theatre critic Robert Cushman. This is a free digital resource spanning over forty years of British and Canadian theatre criticism.

The archive currently features more than 2,500 public reviews and essays, including Robert's Shakespeare criticism, Canadian theatre coverage, musical theatre and music writing, and television criticism. Recent self-published work from the original site will join the new archive after launch.

Start with a browse path, or search across the full public archive by title, production, company, city, or person.
